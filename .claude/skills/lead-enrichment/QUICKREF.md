# Quick Reference — Lead Enrichment (HubSpot)

## Comandos rapidos
- `Enriquecer empresa [Nome]`
- `Qualificar lead e calcular fit ICP`
- `Pesquisar dominio [dominio.com]`

## Ferramentas
- HubSpot MCP: `search_crm_objects`, `search_properties`, `manage_crm_objects` (companies/contacts + NOTE)
- Apify MCP: Google Search Scraper, Website Content Crawler

## Mapeamento
- Empresa: name, domain, industry, numberofemployees, annualrevenue, city, description.
- Contato: jobtitle, email, phone.
- Tese/pitch -> `NOTE` associada ao deal.
